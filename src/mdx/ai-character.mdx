The term "AI character" used here refers to a conversational AI that has its own unique personality, often depicted as an anime-style avatar. These AI characters are designed to connect with users in an intimate and engaging manner. They can understand and respond to user input across modalities, offering assistance, entertainment, or simply providing companionship.

If you're interested in contributing to [this note](https://github.com/thenameless7741/thenameless.net/blob/master/src/mdx/ai-character.mdx), feel free to submit a Pull Request.
